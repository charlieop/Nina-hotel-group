Hotels &

Residences

Nina Hospitality manages and operates a diverse portfolio of 5 hotels, 2 residences and 1 university lodge, catering to a range of guest preferences from midscale to upper upscale. Driven by our heartfelt passion to serve, we strive to go above and beyond guest expectations, delivering a unique value proposition across our properties. With 8 locations in Hong Kong, we offer over 2,900 hotel rooms and residences.

As a homegrown Hong Kong brand, we create vibrant spaces that spark connections and inspirations. Guided by our forward-thinking mindset, personalised service and thoughtful surprises ensure that our guests enjoy memorable experiences at every level.

  • Nina Hotel

    Tsuen Wan West

    The hotel is famed for the tallest skyscraper in New Territories that provides a peaceful escape in the city. Home to 689 rooms at Tower 1 and 919 rooms at Tower 2, all 1,608 rooms and suites enjoy vistas of urban cityscape or harbour view back to Hong Kong Island.

  • Nina Hotel

    Island South

    Nestled in the cosy neighbourhood in the Southside, the hotel boasts 432 well-appointed rooms and suites for a comfortable stay. Backdropped by the lush greenery, choose the room of your choice with either mountain or city view to enjoy a quality time.

  • Nina Hotel

    Causeway Bay

    This 37-storey urban hotel offers quality stay for guests with its 275 rooms and suites. Some rooms perched on the top six floors offer view of Victoria Harbour, and the offer of connecting rooms makes a perfect choice for families.

  • Nina Hotel

    Kowloon East

    The hotel offers the most spacious rooms at the heart of Kowloon East. Revel in the 254 rooms and suites with ample space, style and comfort. Connecting rooms are suitable for those hanker for privacy in family trips.

  • Londgewood

    Mong Kok

    Nestled in the vibrant hub of Mong Kok, Hong Kong's bustling shopping district, this boutique hotel ensures a delightful experience for all its guests. With 87 contemporary rooms and suites, it provides all the essentials for a comfortable stay.

  • Londgewood

    Wan Chai

    Lodgewood Wan Chai offers 91 fully-furnished 1- and 2-bedroom serviced apartments that combine convenience and connectivity in the heart of the bustling Wan Chai neighbourhood. Whether it is for short-term or long-term stay, this residence provides a pleasant experience for any stay.

  • The Lily

     

    Overlooking Repulse Bays golden beach, the curving form of The Lily is another icon in the diverse Nina portfolio. An iconic premier residential property consists of 100 spacious apartments, every resident benefits from the refined comfort and the unique Nina experience. The Lily is elegant and flexible Southside living at its very best. Leasing enquiry: +852 2500 7554
